WaifuEngine Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

WaifuEngine does not collect any personal information. We do not access your name, email, location, browsing history, or any sensitive data.

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect data, we do not use it for any purpose. WaifuEngine simply displays a cute, interactive anime character on your desktop for entertainment.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any user information to third parties. There is no advertising or tracking in this extension.

4. Permissions

  • storage – to remember the character’s position on your screen.
  • activeTab and scripting – to inject the overlay on web pages if needed.

These permissions are strictly used to support the extension’s functionality. No personal data is collected.

5. Remote Code

WaifuEngine does not use remote code. All scripts and files are included in the extension package.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the extension. Updates will be posted on this page.
